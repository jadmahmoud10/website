Admissions

Welcome to the admissions page of the National Online School of Galileo Galilei. Here you will find all the information you need to apply to our esteemed institution.

Application Process

To apply, please follow these steps:

  1. 1. Fill out the online application form using our Admissions Portal .
  2. 2. Submit required documents.
  3. 3. Attend an admission interview (if required).

Requirements

  • 1. Completed application form
  • 2. Proof of previous education
  • 3. Personal statement
  • 4. Letters of recommendation
