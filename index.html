Message from the Management

The National Online School of Galileo Galilei is a virtual school based in Syria, aiming to deliver quality online education to students across Syria and beyond. We offer a comprehensive learning environment in English while also teaching Arabic, Syrian Geography, History, and National Studies. Our goal is to integrate technology seamlessly into education and prepare students for the future. Apply Now to join our journey!

- May Kheder, General Principal
